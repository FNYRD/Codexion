====================
SIMPLE CASES
====================

1) FIFO simple: 3 coders, 1 compile each. Should finish normally.
./codexion 3 1000 100 50 50 1 10 fifo
./codexion 3 1000 100 50 50 1 10 fifo | grep -c "is compiling"
Expected count: 3

2) EDF simple: 3 coders, 1 compile each. Should finish normally.
./codexion 3 1000 100 50 50 1 10 edf
./codexion 3 1000 100 50 50 1 10 edf | grep -c "is compiling"
Expected count: 3

3) FIFO medium: 5 coders, 2 compiles each. Should finish normally.
./codexion 5 1500 100 50 50 2 10 fifo
./codexion 5 1500 100 50 50 2 10 fifo | grep -c "is compiling"
Expected count: 10

4) EDF medium: 5 coders, 2 compiles each. Should finish normally.
./codexion 5 1500 100 50 50 2 10 edf
./codexion 5 1500 100 50 50 2 10 edf | grep -c "is compiling"
Expected count: 10

5) Check dongle logs: each compile must take 2 dongles.
./codexion 5 1500 100 50 50 2 10 fifo
./codexion 5 1500 100 50 50 2 10 fifo | grep -c "has taken a dongle"
Expected count: 20

====================
BURNOUT CASES
====================

11) Burnout forced: compile time is longer than burnout limit. Should print burnout.
./codexion 5 80 100 50 50 2 10 fifo
./codexion 5 80 100 50 50 2 10 fifo | grep -c "burned out"
Expected count: 1 done

12) Burnout forced in EDF. Should print burnout.
./codexion 5 80 100 50 50 2 10 edf
./codexion 5 80 100 50 50 2 10 edf | grep -c "burned out"
Expected count: 1 done

13) Tight burnout with many coders. Should print burnout.
./codexion 10 120 100 50 50 3 10 fifo
./codexion 10 120 100 50 50 3 10 fifo | grep -c "burned out"
Expected count: 1 done

14) No burnout expected: burnout limit safely larger than cycle pressure.
./codexion 5 2000 100 50 50 2 10 fifo
./codexion 5 2000 100 50 50 2 10 fifo | grep -c "burned out"
Expected count: 0 done

15) Burnout precision visual check. Burnout should appear close to time_to_burnout.
./codexion 3 150 200 20 20 2 10 fifo

====================
INVALID INPUTS
====================

16) Missing argument. Should fail and print nothing or return error.
./codexion 3 1000 100 50 50 1 fifo

17) Invalid scheduler. Should fail.
./codexion 3 1000 100 50 50 1 10 random

18) Zero number_of_coders. Should fail.
./codexion 0 1000 100 50 50 1 10 fifo

19) Zero time_to_burnout. Should fail.
./codexion 3 0 100 50 50 1 10 fifo

20) Negative value. Should fail.
./codexion 3 -1000 100 50 50 1 10 fifo

21) Non numeric value. Should fail.
./codexion 3 abc 100 50 50 1 10 fifo

22) Number with letters. Should fail.
./codexion 3 1000 100x 50 50 1 10 fifo

23) Leading zero. Should fail if your verifier rejects leading zeroes.
./codexion 03 1000 100 50 50 1 10 fifo

24) Int overflow in number_of_coders. Should fail.
./codexion 2147483648 1000 100 50 50 1 10 fifo

25) Long overflow in time_to_burnout. Should fail.
./codexion 3 9223372036854775808 100 50 50 1 10 fifo
